Hello! I’m Muhammad Fawad

With a Ph.D. in Mathematical Statistics, my research focuses on health data science, combining statistical modeling, machine learning, and epidemiology to address real-world health challenges, from disease prediction to public health strategy.

I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Tampere University, Finland. Previously, I worked at Zhejiang University and Zhengzhou University, China, where I contributed to research in healthcare data science, infectious disease epidemiology, and predictive modeling using UK Biobank and hospital clinical data.

Research Interests

  • Health data science and healthcare analytics
  • Machine learning for disease prediction and early detection
  • Cardiometabolic disease and cancer epidemiology
  • Infectious disease epidemiology and public health strategy
  • Statistical modeling of complex and large-scale biomedical data

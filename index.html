Hello! I’m Muhammad Fawad

I am a postdoctoral researcher working in mathematical statistics, machine learning, and health data science. My current research focuses on large-scale healthcare data, including cardiometabolic disease, cancer, and public health outcomes.

I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Tampere University, Finland. Previously, I worked at Zhejiang University and Zhengzhou University, China, where I contributed to research in UK Biobank, healthcare data science, infectious disease epidemiology, and predictive modeling.

I earned my Ph.D. in Mathematical Statistics from Central China Normal University, China.

Research Interests

  • Health data science and healthcare analytics
  • Machine learning in medicine
  • Cardiometabolic disease and cancer research
  • Infectious disease epidemiology
  • Statistical modeling of complex data

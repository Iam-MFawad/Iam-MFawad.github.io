About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher specializing in mathematical statistics, machine learning, and health data science. My research focuses on large-scale health data, including cardiometabolic disease, cancer, and public health outcomes. I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Tampere University, Finland.

Research Interests

  • Health data science
  • Machine learning in medicine
  • Cardiometabolic disease and cancer research
  • Infectious disease epidemiology
  • Statistical modeling
